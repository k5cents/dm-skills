## Backgrounds (`backgrounds/<name>.md`)

| `sage.md` | Con, Int, Wis | Skilled |
| `soldier.md` | Str, Dex, Con | Savage Attacker |

See `backgrounds/README.md` (or `origins/README.md`) for the full structure explanation and how ability score grants work.
