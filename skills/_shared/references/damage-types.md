# Damage Types Reference
*Derived from the Systems Reference Document v5.2.1 (CC BY 4.0)*

Thirteen damage types exist in D&D 5e. Resistance, immunity, and vulnerability
apply per type — not per source.

---

## Physical (nonmagical)
| Type | Common sources | Bypassed by |
|------|---------------|-------------|
| **Bludgeoning** | Clubs, falls, cave-ins | Magic weapons, adamantine weapons |
| **Piercing** | Arrows, spears, bites | Magic weapons |
| **Slashing** | Swords, claws, axes | Magic weapons |

Most monsters resistant/immune to B/P/S specify "nonmagical" — magic weapons bypass this.

## Elemental
| Type | Common sources | Notable immunities |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Fire** | Fireball, dragon breath, torches | Fire elementals, red dragons, salamanders |
| **Cold** | Ice Storm, cone of cold, white dragon breath | Ice devils, white dragons, winter wolves |
| **Lightning** | Lightning Bolt, blue dragon breath, storm spells | Air elementals, blue dragons, storm giants |
| **Thunder** | Thunderwave, Shatter, sonic attacks | — (few immunities) |
| **Acid** | Acid Splash, black dragon breath, black pudding | Black puddings, oozes |
| **Poison** | Poison Spray, poisoned weapons, venom | Constructs, undead (universally immune) |

## Arcane / Magical
| Type | Common sources | Notable immunities |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Radiant** | Sacred Flame, divine smites, Dawn | Undead (often vulnerable), radiant-immune fiends |
| **Necrotic** | Inflict Wounds, shadow magic, undead attacks | Undead (immune), constructs (immune) |
| **Psychic** | Mind Blast, Telepathy, psionic attacks | Constructs, oozes (immune) |
| **Force** | Magic Missile, Eldritch Blast, Wall of Force | Almost nothing immune — most reliable damage type |

## Special
| Type | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Radiant** | Often causes vulnerability in undead (GM option) |
| **Force** | Only a handful of creatures (e.g. Helmed Horror) resist it |

---

## Resistance Patterns by Creature Type

| Creature Type | Common resistances | Common immunities |
|--------------|-------------------|------------------|
| Undead | — | Poison, Necrotic (varies) |
| Constructs | Poison, Psychic | — |
| Fiends (demon) | Cold, Fire, Lightning | Poison, Fire (varies by type) |
| Fiends (devil) | Cold | Fire, Poison |
| Elementals | Varies by element | Poison, own element |
| Dragons | — | Own element (breath type) |
| Fey | — | — |

---

## In Eberron (WiC context)
- Warforged: immune to poison and disease, resistant to nonmagical B/P/S (varies by stat block)
- Undead are common in Karrnath and the Mournland — radiant and fire are effective
- Aberrations (daelkyr creations): typically no resistances but high HP pools
