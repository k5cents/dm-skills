## Encounter Multipliers (Action Economy)

Apply to **total monster XP** before comparing to party thresholds.

| # Monsters | Multiplier |
|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | ×1 |
| 2 | ×1.5 |
| 3–6 | ×2 |
| 7–10 | ×2.5 |
| 11–14 | ×3 |
| 15+ | ×4 |

**For small parties (1–2 PCs):** move up one multiplier bracket.
**For large parties (6+ PCs):** move down one bracket.

## Quick Encounter Guidelines

**Deadly ≠ TPK.** Deadly means at least one PC risks death. Hard encounters cost significant resources. Medium is a reasonable default for most fights.

**Action economy rule of thumb:** more monsters = more dangerous per XP than a single large monster. A CR 5 creature (1,800 XP) is less threatening than three CR 2 creatures (450 × 3 = 1,350 XP × 2 multiplier = 2,700 adjusted XP).

**Boss + minions pattern:** 1 significant monster (CR = party level) + 2–6 minions (CR = party level – 3 or lower) creates interesting fights where PCs can split focus.

**Legendary creatures:** treat as 1 multiplier level higher (more threatening than XP suggests) due to Legendary Actions providing extra action economy.
